About Us

Founded by a group of nerdy friends, Geekish Delights servers warm, fresh pastries daily along with specially crafted coffees, teas, and more.

We pride ourselves on being a place were anyone and everyone can come to join our welcoming community.

Our mission is to bring people together and find community in the things we all love.
